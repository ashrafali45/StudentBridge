## Communication

Our team will communicate regularly using messaging platforms such as KakaoTalk and GitHub to discuss progress, share updates, and solve problems.

## Meetings

The team will hold weekly meetings to review progress, discuss challenges, and plan upcoming tasks. Additional meetings may be scheduled if necessary.

## Responsibilities

Each team member is responsible for completing assigned tasks on time and contributing actively to the project. All members should participate in discussions and decision-making.

## Work Contribution

All project work, including code, documentation, and updates, will be shared through the GitHub repository. Team members should commit their work regularly.

## Respect and Collaboration

Team members will respect each other’s ideas and opinions. We will work together collaboratively and support each other to achieve the project goals.

## Conflict Resolution

If disagreements arise, the team will discuss the issue openly and try to reach a fair solution. If necessary, the team leader or instructor can help resolve conflicts.

## Accountability

If a team member is unable to complete a task on time, they must inform the team as early as possible so adjustments can be made.
